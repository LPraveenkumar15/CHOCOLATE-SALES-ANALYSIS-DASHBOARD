# CHOCOLATE-SALES-ANALYSIS-DASHBOARD
Recommended Structure and Order
1. Project Title / Headline
A concise, descriptive name for the dashboard.
Example:
🍫 Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard
A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze chocolate sales performance across different countries, products, salespersons, and time periods. The dashboard helps businesses understand customer preferences, revenue trends, and product profitability through data-driven insights.

2. Short Description / Purpose
1–2 sentences explaining what the dashboard does and why it exists.
Example:
The Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard is an analytical Power BI report designed to monitor and evaluate chocolate product sales performance. It enables businesses to track revenue, profit, customer demand, and regional sales trends to support smarter business decisions and improve overall sales strategy.

3. Tech Stack
List the key technologies used to build the dashboard.
Example:
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for dashboard creation.
🧹 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
📈 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and business logic.
🗂️ Data Modeling – Relationships created among sales, products, customers, and regional tables for analysis.
📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

5. Data Source
Information about where the data comes from and how it is structured.
Example:
Source: Chocolate sales dataset containing transaction-level sales records.
The dataset includes information such as:
Product names
Sales amount
Profit and cost
Country/Region
Salesperson details
Order date and month
Customer information
Quantity sold
The data is structured into multiple related tables to support filtering, trend analysis, and interactive reporting.

6. Features / Highlights
The best dashboard explanation format:
Business problem
Goal of the dashboard
Walkthrough of key visuals
Business impact & insights
Example:
• Business Problem
Chocolate companies generate huge amounts of sales data every day, but businesses often struggle to identify:
Which products perform best
Which regions generate the highest revenue
Seasonal sales patterns
Salesperson performance
Profitability across products
Without proper visualization and analysis, decision-making becomes difficult and inefficient.
• Goal of the Dashboard

The main objectives of this dashboard are:

To analyze overall chocolate sales performance
To identify top-selling products and profitable categories
To monitor monthly and yearly sales trends
To compare regional and country-wise sales performance
To evaluate salesperson contribution and efficiency
To support data-driven business decisions
• Walkthrough of Key Visuals
📌 KPI Cards

Displays overall business performance metrics such as:

Total Sales
Total Profit
Total Orders
Quantity Sold
📌 Sales Trend Analysis

Line charts and area charts are used to show:

Monthly sales growth
Yearly revenue trends
Seasonal demand fluctuations
📌 Product Performance Analysis

Bar charts and treemaps help identify:

Top-selling chocolate products
Most profitable products
Low-performing categories
📌 Regional Sales Analysis

Maps and column charts display:

Country-wise sales distribution
Regional revenue comparison
Highest demand locations
📌 Salesperson Performance

Tables and charts analyze:

Top-performing salespersons
Revenue contribution by employee
Individual sales targets and achievements
• Business Impact & Insights

The dashboard provides valuable business insights such as:

Identification of high-demand chocolate products
Better inventory and stock planning
Improved regional sales strategies
Enhanced marketing decision-making
Increased profitability through data analysis
Faster and more accurate reporting process
